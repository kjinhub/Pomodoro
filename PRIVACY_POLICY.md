# Privacy Policy

Tomato Pomodoro does not collect personal information.

The app stores timer settings, local session counts, selected premium asset ID, and locally cached purchase entitlement data on the device. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play Billing. The app does not send personal data to an external server.

Premium asset purchases may require Google Play services and are subject to Google's payment and account processing.
